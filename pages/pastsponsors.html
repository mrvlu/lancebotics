Past Sponsors

We would like to thank all the sponsors that have donated and sponsored the Lancebotics 200 Robotics Team. It is safe to say that without their support, we would not have been able to have advanced as far as we did, nor would we be able to support our members’ continued interest in STEM.

If you are an older sponsor and are interested or wish to sponsor us again, please read the sponsorship package (found both on the bottom of this page, and the home page). Alternatively, if you have any questions, or would like to contact us, please view the Contact page by clicking here.

Unfortunately, the resources that are required to compete in the 2016 - 2017 Vex Robotics Competition season are not free, which is why we rely on the generosity of our sponsors to facilitate the building of robots that compete against other organisations or schools. Some of resources required to ensure we stay as world-class team are listed below:

  • Event Registration
  • Components/Hardware
  • Tools
  • Software Licenses
  • Field and Game Pieces

With this support, Lancebotics as a team can continue to build high quality robots that will be able to compete with the Vex Worlds’ best, as well as aim for the number one spot this season.
