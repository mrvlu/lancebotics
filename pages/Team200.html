What is Lancebotics?

Established in 2013, LanceBotics is Leaside High Schoolâ€™s Vex Robotics club team. Encompassing three teams, LanceBotics ultimate goal is to represent Leaside (and Canada) internationally, and to do the very best job possible. Team 200A has made it to the Vex Robotics World Championships in 2014- 2015, with a second visitation in 2015- 2016. The aim of all three teams is to be able to compete in the 2016 - 2017 VEX Worlds Robotics competition season.

Lancebotics is proud to have represented Canada internationally two years running at the annual Vex Robotics World Championship Louisville, Kentucky. We credit our successes to our generous sponsors and hard working team members. Our perseverance and dedication to overcoming the challenges that come with participating in VEX Robotics make us the team to support. With their help, we hope we can make it to the VEX World Championships for an unprecedented third year in a row.

How can I join Lancebotics?

Unfortunately, applications for joining Lancebotics are currently closed. However, if you are still interested and wish to join this year, please contact Mr. Lu by clicking here. Please note that as we are a high school team, this offer is only to those who attend Leaside High School or are apart of the Leaside Community.

Applications open in June through to September. Team interviews follow after all applications are gathered in September. Stay tuned to find out where to apply on the website.
