Team 200B

Est. 2015

Team B consists of 18 members bringing their own unique traits to the one common interest: the fascination of robotics. Team B is a new addition to the Lancebotics family, even though the team is the least experienced with Lancebotics and VEX, they show the passion and drive needed to overcome any problems they may face. In the current stages, Team B has made progress. They currently have a good portion of the robot completed (this includes the robot's base, and a shooting mechanism to launch the balls in the competition.) Although the team is hard at work there is more to be done, however this team does show promise, and is a very good addition to the Lancebotics family.
