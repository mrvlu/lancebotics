Team 200A

Est. 2013

Made up of most of last year's team, along with a handful of experienced grade 12 newcomers, Lancebotics team 200A serves not only as a role model for the younger, less experienced Lancebotics teams to look up to, but a paragon for success as well, having bested the odds during the 2014-15 season and making it to the VEX Worlds in Louisville, Kentucky. Whilst acting as mentors, Lancebotics team 200A is no less ambitious in their endeavours for the 2015-16 season. With construction of their robot well underway, team 200A seems to be on track for their first competition, which is scheduled to take place on the 28th of November. As their first ordeal slowly approaches, the pressure is afoot for each and every member of the team to perform to the very best of their ability. For many, this includes devoting large quantities of time both at school and at home to the development of the robots, media assets and the gruelling task of acquiring sponsorships in hopes of once again making it to the international level competition. Keep up the hard work 200A!
